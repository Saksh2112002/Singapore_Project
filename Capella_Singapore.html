About Capella Singapore

Set on 30 acres of lush rainforest, Capella Singapore blends colonial charm with contemporary design. This luxury resort is known for its serene ambiance and exceptional hospitality.

Enjoy award-winning dining, holistic wellness experiences, and elegant villas and suites offering breathtaking views of the South China Sea.
