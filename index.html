What Not to Do in Singapore

Stay respectful, stay informed — avoid these common mistakes when visiting Singapore.

🚫

Do not litter, spit, or forget to flush in public. These are strictly enforced.

No Littering

Public Cleanliness Rule
🍬

Chewing gum is restricted. Don’t bring or use it unless medically approved.

No Gum Chewing

Import Restriction
🚷

Jaywalking is illegal. Always cross roads at pedestrian crossings.

Don’t Jaywalk

Traffic Law
🚉

Eating or drinking on MRT trains and stations is not allowed.

No Eating in MRT

Public Transit Rule
🚭

Smoking in public areas like malls and parks is prohibited.

No Smoking

Smoke Zones Only
💊

Drug laws are extremely strict — possession or trafficking can mean the death penalty.

No Drugs

Zero Tolerance
