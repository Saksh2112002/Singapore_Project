About Ibis Bencoolen

Located near Bugis MRT, Ibis Singapore on Bencoolen is a budget-friendly hotel offering smart comfort and sustainability. Ideal for both business and leisure travelers, it’s walking distance to shopping, museums, and temples.

Enjoy modern rooms, a 24-hour restaurant, eco-friendly initiatives, and easy access to Orchard Road, Suntec, and Marina Bay areas.
