About Raffles Hotel

Established in 1887, Raffles Hotel Singapore is an iconic colonial-style luxury hotel that exudes timeless charm and heritage. It's known for its world-class hospitality, history, and elegance.

Home to the legendary Long Bar and birthplace of the Singapore Sling, Raffles offers a unique blend of tradition and modern refinement with beautifully restored suites and lush tropical gardens.
