🌊 Wild Wild Wet

Located in Downtown East, Wild Wild Wet is one of Singapore’s largest and most popular water parks. Perfect for families, thrill-seekers, and anyone looking to beat the tropical heat, the park offers an exciting blend of high-speed slides, relaxing lazy rivers, and splash zones for all ages.

📍 Location: 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599

🚇 How to Get There: Take the MRT to Pasir Ris Station (EW1) and walk about 10 minutes to Downtown East.

🎢 Attractions

  • Ular-Lah (Wild Wild Wet):


    📍 Location: Wild Wild Wet, 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599
    🎢 Overview: Ular-Lah is Singapore’s first raft slide, designed to give you the thrill of white-water rafting. With sharp turns and steep plunges, it’s perfect for groups of 4–6 people.
    🌊 Ride Features:
    • Large family-sized rafts that spin and splash down the slide.
    • Simulates a white-water river rafting adventure.
    • Great for team fun and laughter.
    🧒 Rider Info: Riders must be at least 1.22m tall to participate. Group ride only.
    🕐 Queue Times: Popular ride — best to visit early before crowds build up.
    🌟 Tips:
    • Hold tight! The raft spins quickly on the way down.
    • Coordinate your group’s weight for balanced sliding.
    • Use the express pass on weekends to skip long lines.

  • Torpedo (Wild Wild Wet):


    📍 Location: 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599 (Inside Downtown East)
    🎢 Overview: Torpedo is Wild Wild Wet’s most intense thrill ride. Step into a transparent capsule, hear the countdown, and brace yourself as the floor disappears beneath you—sending you plummeting straight down a high-speed tunnel.
    💥 Ride Highlights:
    • 6-storey vertical free-fall drop
    • Speed of up to 70km/h
    • Enclosed see-through slide sections
    • Heart-racing twists and turns
    🧒 Rider Info: Minimum height: 1.22m. Not suitable for those with heart conditions.
    🕐 Queue Tips: Arrive before noon for shorter wait times.
    🌟 Pro Tips:
    • Take a deep breath before launch—it’s fast and intense!
    • Wear snug swimwear—this is a high-velocity ride.
    • Use the nearby lockers for phones and accessories.

  • Vortex (Wild Wild Wet):


    📍 Location: 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599 (Inside Downtown East)
    🎢 Overview: Vortex is a high-speed enclosed body slide that spins and loops you through a thrilling 360° spiral before splashing out. It's perfect for adrenaline junkies who love fast, disorienting fun.
    🚀 Ride Highlights:
    • Enclosed spiral tunnel slide
    • 360-degree loops and sharp twists
    • Sudden drops and splash exits
    • Single-rider body slide (no floats)
    🧒 Rider Info: Minimum height: 1.22m. Must be able to swim. No life jackets allowed on this ride.
    🕐 Best Time to Ride: Late afternoon when crowds are lighter.
    🌟 Pro Tips:
    • Cross your arms and legs for maximum speed and safety.
    • Keep your head back to reduce water splash in the face.
    • Store all items in lockers—nothing is allowed during the ride.

  • Shiok River (Wild Wild Wet):


    📍 Location: 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599 (Inside Downtown East)
    🌀 Overview: Shiok River offers a peaceful contrast to the high-speed thrills of other rides. Float along a gentle lazy river that circles much of the park, letting you relax and enjoy the tropical vibes.
    💧 River Features:
    • Lazy river loop around the waterpark
    • Gentle current – ideal for all ages
    • Multiple entry and exit points
    • Inflatable floats provided
    🧒 Rider Info: Suitable for all ages. Children under 1.22m must be accompanied by an adult.
    🕐 Best Time to Ride: Late morning or after lunch for a relaxing float.
    🌟 Pro Tips:
    • Use waterproof sunscreen – the ride is mostly open-air.
    • Float with friends or family for a fun group experience.
    • Pause under the waterfalls for a quick splash.

  • Kids Zone (Wild Wild Wet):


    📍 Location: 1 Pasir Ris Close, Singapore 519599 (Inside Downtown East)
    👶 Overview: Specially designed for toddlers and young children, the Kids Zone offers safe water play with mini slides, shallow pools, and interactive fountains. It’s a paradise for the little ones!
    🎠 Attractions:
    • Yippee: Mini splash pool with colorful water jets
    • Kidz Zone: Scaled-down slides for younger kids
    • Interactive fountains and tipping buckets
    • Shaded play areas and soft flooring for safety
    🧒 Rider Info: Ideal for ages 18 months to 8 years. Parental supervision required.
    🕐 Best Time to Visit: Early morning or late afternoon when it’s cooler.
    🌟 Pro Tips:
    • Bring swim diapers for toddlers (mandatory).
    • Use shaded seating nearby to keep an eye on your kids comfortably.
    • Pack extra clothes and snacks — kids won’t want to leave!

🍴 Food & Amenities

  • On-site dining: Local and international dishes available inside the park.
  • Shower facilities, lockers, and rental cabanas available.
  • Retail shop for swimwear, goggles, and souvenirs.

🕐 Opening Hours: 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM (Weekdays), 11:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Weekends & Holidays)

🎫 Tickets: Prices vary by age and day; discounted rates for locals and online bookings available.

🌟 Tips for Visitors

  • Arrive early on weekends to avoid long queues.
  • Book tickets online for the best deals.
  • Apply waterproof sunscreen and bring a dry change of clothes.
  • Use e-lockers for convenience.
